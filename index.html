About me

I'm Candy Mzungu, a curious and enthusiastic software developer at the start of my journey in the tech world. My passion for technology is fueled by a love for problem-solving, creativity, ... and the thrill of bringing ideas to life through code. I may be a beginner in software development, but my curiosity and eagerness to learn have led me to dive deep into the world of programming. Whether it's building a simple web app or tinkering with new technologies, I'm always excited to take on new challenges and grow my skills. I've been learning and experimenting with various programming languages and frameworks, from HTML and CSS to JavaScript and React. The journey has been exhilarating, and I'm constantly amazed at how much there is to discover in this ever-evolving field. While coding is a big part of my life, I'm also someone who loves to stay active and explore my creative side. When I'm not in front of a screen, you can find me:

Swimming: There's something about the rhythm of the water that helps me clear my mind and find new perspectives. Swimming is my go-to activity when I need a break from the digital world.
Drawing: Art has always been a passion of mine. Whether it's sketching characters, doodling landscapes, or just letting my imagination run wild, drawing allows me to express myself in ways that words can't capture.
Outdoor Adventures: From hiking in the mountains to camping under the stars, I find peace and inspiration in the great outdoors. There's nothing like a breath of fresh air and the beauty of nature to recharge my batteries.
